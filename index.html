    13. 1. Dashboard

    The doctor can log in and out of the system using the button on the top right corner. Doctor also see

    • All Patients
    • Upcoming Appointments
    • Total Sessions
    • Total Earnings on dashboard

      • There are several options that the doctor uses on the left.

    2. Appointments

    During appointments, the doctor has access to the patient's name, symptoms, appointment ID, date, time, status, and action in addition to seeing every appointment.

    3. Schedule

    A doctor can set the day and hour of their appointments using the schedule feature.

    4. Labs

    In labs there are many option like

    • Order Labs: In order labs doctor can buy online lab test.
    • Patient Reports: In Patient reports doctor can see patient lab test reports.
    • Pending Online Lab: Within the pending online lab, the physician can view any lab test that has not been approved by them. They can also choose to approve or cancel the test, and there is a "view patient" button that allows them to view the patient's whole medical history.
    • Approved Labs: In approved labs doctor can see all lab test which doctor approve.
    • My Pending Requisitions: A doctor who purchases an online lab test from website without receiving approval from another doctor can view the request for it in my pending requisition.
    • My Requisitions: In my requisition doctor can see all lab test requisitions.
